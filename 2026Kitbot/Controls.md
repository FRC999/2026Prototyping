##Heres the button layout for the Kitbot

L Trigger:
Intakes fuel into the hopper

R Trigger:
Takes fuel in hopper and sends to shooter and shoots the fuel

R Bumber:
Intakes fuel and immediately shoots the fuel

A:
Reverses shooter motor to unjam the shooter but doesnt move the hopper motor(doesnt affect hopper)

B:
Empties the hopper by reversing all intake motors
